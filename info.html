Story

A deadly computer virus has taken over the system, corrupting everything in its path. If left unchecked, it will spread beyond the digital world, causing massive destruction.

You are the system's last line of defense — a user armed with a throwable laptop, fighting to eliminate the threat.

Your mission is to clear each infected stage, destroy all virus enemies, and defeat the powerful virus bosses standing in your way. As you progress, you’ll gain stronger upgrades to prepare for the ultimate battle against the Final Virus, the source of the corruption.

Goal

The main goal of Crash Buster is to clear all stages, defeat virus enemies and bosses, and destroy the Final Virus to restore the system.

Objectives

  • Progress through all stages by eliminating waves of computer viruses.
  • Defeat virus bosses that appear at the end of certain stages.
  • Collect upgrades to strengthen your throwable laptop and abilities.
  • Survive and fight strategically to avoid being overwhelmed by enemies.
  • Destroy the Final Virus in the last stage to complete the game and save the system.
